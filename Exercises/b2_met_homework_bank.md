## 3. Short Answer, 10 Items

**Instructions:** Answer each question in 2 to 4 sentences.

### Item 1
What is one advantage of using technology in education?

**Sample answer:** One advantage is that technology gives students more flexible access to materials. For example, they can review lessons, watch explanations, and practice at their own pace.

### Item 2
Describe a time when you had to solve a problem at work or school.

**Sample answer:** Last month, my team had a scheduling conflict before an important meeting. I contacted everyone, suggested two new times, and helped the group choose the best option.

### Item 3
Do you think public transportation should be improved in large cities? Why?

**Sample answer:** Yes, public transportation should be improved because it reduces traffic and helps people save money. It is especially important for workers who cannot drive or afford a car.

### Item 4
What makes a person a good team member?

**Sample answer:** A good team member listens carefully, respects other people’s ideas, and completes tasks on time. Teamwork becomes easier when everyone communicates clearly.

### Item 5
Should people work from home permanently? Explain your opinion.

**Sample answer:** I think working from home is useful, but it should not be permanent for everyone. Some people are more productive at home, while others need face-to-face communication.

### Item 6
What is one environmental problem in your community?

**Sample answer:** One problem is that many people do not recycle properly. The city could solve this by providing clearer instructions and more recycling bins.

### Item 7
How can adults manage stress during exam preparation?

**Sample answer:** Adults can manage stress by creating a realistic study schedule and taking short breaks. They should also practice regularly instead of studying everything at the last minute.

### Item 8
Why is clear communication important in healthcare?

**Sample answer:** Clear communication is important because mistakes can affect patient safety. Nurses and doctors need to explain instructions in a way patients can understand.

### Item 9
Would you prefer to study alone or in a group? Why?

**Sample answer:** I prefer studying alone when I need to focus, but group study is useful for speaking practice. Both methods can help depending on the goal.

### Item 10
What should a city do to make public spaces safer?

**Sample answer:** A city should improve lighting, maintain parks, and increase community programs. These actions can make people feel more comfortable using public spaces.

## 4. Speaking Prompts, 10 Items

**Instructions:** Prepare a 60 to 90 second answer.

### Prompt 1, Image Description

**Tip:** Describe the setting first, then the people, then make one inference.

**Tip:** Use past tense clearly: situation, action, result.

### Prompt 6, Image Description

## 12. Sentence Level-Up, 10 Items

**Instructions:** Improve the basic sentence to make it stronger and more B2-level.

# Platform Generation Notes

Use this structure when generating new B2 MET homework packs:

- Fill in the Blank should use short paragraphs, not isolated sentences.
- Listening should use longer scripts with three questions each.
- Short Dialogues should include 4 to 6 lines and one comprehension question.
- Open-answer exercises should include sample answers for teacher reference.
- Objective exercises should include clear answer keys.
- Topics should be realistic for adult MET candidates.
- Keep vocabulary strong B2, not C1.
- Avoid childish topics and overly academic language.
